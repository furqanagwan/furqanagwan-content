## Nutrition (per wrap)

- **Calories:** 335
- **Protein:** 22g
- **Carbs:** 29g
- **Fat:** 14g

## Ingredients

- **2 eggs**  
  _Brand:_ British Lion Eggs
- **1 tortilla**  
  _Brand:_ Warburtons
- **30g cheddar**  
  _Brand:_ Cathedral City
- **10ml milk**  
  _Brand:_ Cravendale
- **Pinch salt & pepper**
- **5g butter or spray**  
  _Brand:_ Frylight

## Instructions

1. Whisk eggs, milk, salt, pepper.
2. Cook eggs in pan with butter.
3. Warm tortilla.
4. Fill wrap with eggs, cheese. Roll and serve.
