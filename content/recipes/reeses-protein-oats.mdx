## Nutrition (per jar)

- **Calories:** 410  
- **Protein:** 32g  
- **Carbohydrates:** 36g  
- **Fat:** 16g  

_Based on: Quaker Oats, chocolate whey, SKIPPY Peanut Butter, Reese’s Cups, Tesco Semi-Skimmed Milk._

## Ingredients

- **40g** Quaker Rolled Oats  
- **30g** chocolate whey protein  
- **120g** Fage Total 0% Greek Yogurt  
- **100ml** semi-skimmed milk  
- **10g** SKIPPY Peanut Butter  
- **1** Reese’s Peanut Butter Cup (approx. 10g), chopped  
- Optional: dash of vanilla, pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. In a jar or bowl, **combine oats, whey, yogurt, milk, and peanut butter**.
2. Stir thoroughly and refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours).
3. Before serving, **top with chopped Reese’s Cup** and optional add-ins.

## Serving Ideas

- Top with cacao nibs or crushed peanuts
- Use almond milk and sugar-free chocolate to lower sugar

### Portioning Tip

Yields 1 large jar (~350g total weight). Use a kitchen scale for accuracy when topping.

> _All macros calculated using UK food labels: Quaker, SKIPPY, Reese’s, Fage, Tesco milk, and whey listed. Substitute values may differ._

> **Allergy/Intolerance Warning:**  
> Contains milk (yogurt, whey, milk), peanuts, soy (Reese’s), and possible traces of tree nuts.  
> Oats may contain gluten unless certified GF.  
> Always read labels and consult a professional for allergy-safe substitutions.
