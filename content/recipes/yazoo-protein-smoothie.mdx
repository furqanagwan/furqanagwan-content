## Nutrition (per smoothie)

- **Calories:** 380  
- **Protein:** 34g  
- **Carbohydrates:** 38g  
- **Fat:** 9g  

_Nutrition based on Yazoo chocolate milk, banana, MyProtein whey._

## Ingredients

- **200ml** Yazoo Chocolate Milk (reduced sugar if possible)  
- **1/2** frozen banana  
- **30g** chocolate or vanilla whey protein  
- **Handful** ice cubes  
- Optional: 5g cocoa powder for richness

## Instructions

1. Blend everything on high until smooth.
2. Serve cold â€” top with crushed ice or grated chocolate if desired.

## Serving Ideas

- Add oats to make it a breakfast smoothie
- Use Yazoo strawberry with vanilla whey for a fruity version

### Portioning Tip

Final blend yields ~300ml. Adjust milk-to-ice ratio for thickness.
