## Nutrition (per parfait)

- **Calories:** 330  
- **Protein:** 30g  
- **Carbohydrates:** 24g  
- **Fat:** 13g  

_Nutrition values based on: Fage 0%, hazelnut whey, Kinder Bueno, Tesco Semi-Skimmed Milk._

## Ingredients

- **150g** Fage Total 0% Greek Yogurt  
- **30g** hazelnut or chocolate whey protein  
- **10g** cocoa powder or hazelnut butter (optional)  
- **1** Kinder Bueno (21.5g), chopped  
- **1–2 tsp** semi-skimmed milk (if needed for thinning)

## Instructions

1. **Mix yogurt, whey, and milk** until smooth and fluffy.
2. Layer into a glass: half yogurt, half Kinder, then repeat.
3. Chill for 5 minutes or eat immediately.

## Serving Ideas

- Add roasted hazelnuts or sugar-free chocolate chips
- Make ahead in a jar for a grab-and-go snack

### Portioning Tip

Finished parfait weighs ~220g. Kinder adds ~10g topping per layer — weigh pieces if adjusting.

> _All macros calculated using UK food labels: Fage, MyProtein, Kinder Bueno, Tesco Milk. Adjust if swapping brands._

> Contains milk (yogurt, whey, Kinder), wheat (Kinder wafer), soy lecithin, hazelnuts.
