## Nutrition (per pot)

- **Calories:** 270
- **Protein:** 22g
- **Carbs:** 24g
- **Fat:** 9g

## Ingredients

- **170g Greek yoghurt**  
  _Brand:_ FAGE
- **50g berries**  
  _Brand:_ Tesco
- **15g mixed nuts**  
  _Brand:_ Whitworths
- **1 tsp honey (optional)**  
  _Brand:_ Rowse

## Instructions

1. Add yoghurt to bowl.
2. Top with berries and nuts.
3. Drizzle honey if you like.
